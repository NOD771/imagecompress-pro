How to Compress Images Online

Our free image compressor helps you reduce file sizes while maintaining visual quality. Perfect for web developers, social media managers, and anyone who needs to optimize images.

Why Compress Your Images?

  • Faster websites: Smaller images load quicker, improving user experience and SEO
  • Save storage space: Reduce the size of your image collections
  • Social media ready: Meet platform requirements without quality loss
  • Email attachments: Send images faster with smaller file sizes

Key Features

  • Privacy focused: All processing happens in your browser
  • Multiple formats: Supports JPG, PNG, and modern WEBP format
  • Adjustable quality: Fine-tune the balance between size and quality
  • No registration: Use immediately without signing up
